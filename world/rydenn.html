Ry'denn

rouge/fighter | githyanki | he/him | trans |

With Faerun on the brink of assimilation on one end and destruction in the other, one man amongst the many faces and forces that hold the line against such evil is a man of pure passion. Once a humble urchin from the streets of Waterdeep, Ry'denn carries battles with nothing but bloodlust and excitement, with a knack for being first in line in the face of ever greater threats he had once only heard of in pages.

To stop there would only do a disservice to him, though, as he's as passionate as he is strong in his ideals of loyalty and honor- Ideals of which are upheld even further with the thanks to his warrior in arms and doting partner, Sh'r'kon. Together, they travel across the lands, fighting against treachery in whatever form it takes, carrying with them the enemy's own medicine as his secret weapon; The awesome, ghastly power of Myrkul, the God of Death.
