>early design options





The main points of their designs was for Dennis to have 'puffy pink cotton candy hair' and that he always wears a leather jacket, but otherwise doesn't particularly bother with his outfit. Abby's only point was that she is wearing the same leather jacket as Dennis. The rest was left up to me so I went about creating designs that would work with, and compliment the main piece of both characters; the jacket.

For Dennis, my client went with the puffy hair of the first and the boxier body design. Abby was very quick to come to life, and didn't go through any real revisions.

>finished design




Abby, being a ghost that is teathered to an object, specifically the leather jacket in question, I wanted to have an interesting way to show that visually, so I ended up having the leather straps of their jacket cuffs serve that purpose.
There was also discussion on and ideas about what color to use for Abby, and I did have a version of her as a sort of light pink, but we ended up with the very classic blue green for her. I think it compliments the burgandy jacket and Dennis' cotton candy hair.
